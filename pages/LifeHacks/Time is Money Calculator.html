About This Calculator

The Time is Money Calculator shows the true value of your time. Convert any activity into its hourly wage equivalent and see what you could buy with the time you spend. Make smarter decisions about how you invest your hours.

How It Works

  • Salary Input: Enter your annual salary.
  • Activity & Time: Describe your activity and time spent.
  • Opportunity Cost: See the real cost of your activity.
  • Alternative Purchases: Discover what you could have bought instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my salary data saved?

No, all calculations are local and private.

What if I'm paid hourly?

Enter your annualized income for best results.

Can I compare multiple activities?

Yes, just enter new activities and see the results.

How accurate is this?

It's an estimate—real value depends on your priorities.

Introduction

At Docket One ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website docket.one and use our calculator services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

Calculator Inputs: When you use our calculators, you may input various types of data (financial figures, personal preferences, text for translation, etc.). Important: All calculation data is processed locally in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Communication: If you contact us directly (email, feedback forms), we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • The content of your message
  • Any attachments you send

2. Information Collected Automatically

Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated data about how our Service is used:

  • Which calculators are most popular
  • General geographic region (country/state level only)
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral sources

Technical Information:

  • IP address (anonymized after processing)
  • Browser user agent
  • Screen resolution and device capabilities
  • Performance metrics (page load times, errors)

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifying information (unless voluntarily provided via contact forms)
  • Detailed location data or GPS coordinates
  • Your calculation inputs or results
  • Financial account information
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Health information
  • Biometric data
  • Information about children under 13

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our calculator services
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience
  • Technical Support: To respond to inquiries and provide customer service
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We Do NOT Use Your Information For

  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Targeted advertising or marketing
  • Creating detailed user profiles
  • Cross-device tracking
  • Behavioral analysis for commercial purposes

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Information Only In These Limited Circumstances:

Service Providers: We may share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted service providers who help us operate our Service:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (data is anonymized before transmission)
  • Content Delivery Networks: To improve site performance globally
  • Hosting Providers: To maintain website infrastructure

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

Safety and Rights Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Docket One, our users, or others.

We Do NOT Share For:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Commercial data brokerage
  • Social media integration
  • Third-party analytics beyond Google Analytics
  • Any purpose not explicitly stated above

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server configurations
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls and authentication systems

Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee training on data protection
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Incident response procedures

Your Privacy Rights

🛡️ Your Rights and Controls

Right to Know

You can request information about what personal data we have collected about you.

Right to Delete

You can request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).

Right to Correct

You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.

Right to Opt-Out

You can opt out of non-essential data collection through browser settings.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@docket.one with:

  • Your specific request
  • Verification of your identity
  • Details about the information or action requested

We will respond within 30 days of receiving a valid request.

International Data Transfers

Our Service is hosted in the United States. If you access our Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may be different from those in your country.

For EU/UK Users: We implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers including Standard Contractual Clauses with service providers and additional security measures for sensitive data.

Retention of Information

  • Calculator Data: Processed locally and never stored on our servers
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized usage data retained for 26 months, then automatically deleted
  • Contact Communications: Retained for 3 years for customer service purposes, then deleted
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

Parents/Guardians: If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at privacy@docket.one.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: Request details about personal information collected, used, disclosed, or sold
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information, but you can request to opt out
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

Contact us at privacy@docket.one with "CCPA Request" in the subject line to exercise these rights.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For EU/UK users, we process personal information based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: Analytics and service improvement
  • Consent: Contact communications and optional features
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws

You have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent. Contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@docket.one for GDPR-related inquiries.

  • Minor Changes: Updated policy will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date

Your Continued Use: Continued use of our Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

About This Calculator

The Conspiracy Theory Plausibility Index lets you rate and compare wild theories for fun. Score plausibility, evidence, and entertainment value—then see which ideas rise to the top!

How It Works

  • Enter Theories: Add your favorite or original conspiracy theories.
  • Rate Factors: Score each theory on plausibility, evidence, and fun.
  • Index Output: See a ranked list and visual chart of results.
  • Share: Download or share your index with friends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this serious?

No, it's a lighthearted tool for entertainment.

Can I add my own theories?

Yes, custom entries are supported.

Yes, use the export or share feature.

About This Calculator

The Pizza Pi Calculator helps you get the most pizza for your money. Compare different sizes and prices to find the best value—because in pizza math, bigger is often better!

How It Works

  • Input Sizes & Prices: Enter up to three pizza diameters and prices.
  • Instant Value: See area, crust, and value per dollar for each pizza.
  • Visualization: Compare pizzas visually to spot the best deal.
  • Presets: Use quick buttons for common pizza sizes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this based on real math?

Yes! Area is πr², so a bigger pizza is almost always a better deal.

Can I use metric units?

Enter diameters in inches for best results, but you can convert from cm.

Does it work for square pizzas?

This tool is for round pizzas, but you can estimate.

Is my data saved?

No, all calculations are local and private.
